Important Notice

This notice describes how medical information about you may be used and disclosed and how you can get access to this information. Please review it carefully.

About Stay Dripped IV & Wellness Co.

Stay Dripped IV & Wellness Co. is a healthcare practice providing mobile IV therapy, peptide treatments, hormone therapy, weight management, and telehealth services to patients in Arizona. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your health information.

Covered Entity Information:

  • Practice Name: Stay Dripped IV & Wellness Co.
  • Service Area: Arizona
  • Services: Mobile IV therapy, peptide therapy, hormone therapy, weight management, telehealth consultations
  • Privacy Officer: Available at privacy@staydrippediv.com

How We Use and Disclose Your Health Information

Uses and Disclosures for Treatment, Payment, and Healthcare Operations

We may use and disclose your health information without your written authorization for:

Treatment

  • Providing, coordinating, or managing your healthcare
  • Consulting with other healthcare providers about your care
  • Referring you to other healthcare providers
  • Emergency medical treatment

Example: A nurse may access your medical history to determine the appropriate IV therapy for your condition.

Payment

  • Billing for services provided
  • Processing insurance claims
  • Collection activities
  • Determining insurance coverage

Example: We may submit your treatment information to your insurance company to obtain payment for services.

Healthcare Operations

  • Quality assessment and improvement activities
  • Staff training and education
  • Business planning and management
  • Compliance and regulatory activities

Example: We may review treatment records to evaluate the quality of care and improve our services.

Other Uses and Disclosures

We may also use or disclose your health information in the following situations:

  • Public Health Activities: Disease reporting, FDA adverse event reporting
  • Health Oversight: Audits, investigations, licensing activities
  • Judicial and Administrative Proceedings: Court orders, subpoenas
  • Law Enforcement: When required by law or court order
  • Emergency Situations: To prevent serious threat to health or safety
  • Worker's Compensation: As required by law
  • Business Associates: Third parties who help us operate (with signed agreements)

Your Rights Regarding Your Health Information

Right to Access

You have the right to inspect and obtain a copy of your health information, including medical records and billing records.

  • Request must be made in writing
  • We will respond within 30 days
  • Reasonable copying fees may apply
  • Electronic records can be provided in electronic format

Right to Request Amendment

You have the right to request that we amend your health information if you believe it is incorrect or incomplete.

  • Request must be made in writing with supporting reasons
  • We may deny the request if the information is accurate and complete
  • If denied, you may submit a statement of disagreement

Right to Request Restrictions

You have the right to request restrictions on how we use or disclose your health information.

  • We are not required to agree to all restrictions
  • We must agree to restrict disclosures to health plans if you pay out-of-pocket in full
  • Restrictions do not apply to emergency treatment

Right to Confidential Communications

You have the right to request that we communicate with you about your health information in a certain way or at a certain location.

  • Request must be reasonable
  • You do not need to explain the reason for your request
  • We will accommodate reasonable requests

Right to an Accounting of Disclosures

You have the right to receive a list of disclosures we have made of your health information (excluding disclosures for treatment, payment, healthcare operations, and certain other exceptions).

  • Covers disclosures made in the 6 years prior to your request
  • First accounting in a 12-month period is free
  • Additional requests may incur a reasonable fee

Right to Choose a Personal Representative

You have the right to designate someone to act on your behalf regarding your health information.

  • Personal representative must have legal authority
  • Proper documentation required (power of attorney, court order, etc.)
  • Parents typically serve as personal representatives for minor children

Right to File a Complaint

You have the right to file a complaint if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.

  • File with our Privacy Officer: privacy@staydrippediv.com
  • File with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights
  • No retaliation for filing complaints

Our Duties

We are required by law to:

  • Maintain the privacy and security of your health information
  • Provide you with this Notice of Privacy Practices
  • Follow the terms of this notice
  • Notify you if your health information is breached
  • Not use or disclose your health information other than as described in this notice or as authorized by you

Changes to This Notice

We reserve the right to change this notice and make the new notice effective for all health information we maintain. If we make material changes to this notice, we will:

  • Post the revised notice on our website
  • Make copies available at our practice
  • Provide you with a copy upon request
  • Update the effective date
