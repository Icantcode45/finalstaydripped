About Telehealth Services

Telehealth allows healthcare providers to evaluate, diagnose, and treat patients using interactive audio, video, and electronic communications technologies. This consent form provides important information about telehealth services offered by Stay Dripped IV & Wellness Co.

What is Telehealth?

Telehealth services include:

  • Virtual consultations via secure video conferencing
  • Remote monitoring and follow-up care
  • Electronic transmission of medical information
  • Digital health assessments and treatment planning
  • Prescription services (when clinically appropriate)

Benefits of Telehealth

  • Convenient access to healthcare from your location
  • Reduced travel time and associated costs
  • Continuity of care during illness or isolation
  • Access to specialists and specialized care
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Reduced exposure to infectious diseases

Risks and Limitations of Telehealth

Technical Risks

  • Technology failures, interruptions, or poor connection quality
  • Delays in medical evaluation and treatment
  • Security risks inherent in electronic communications
  • Information transmitted may not be sufficient for decision-making

Clinical Limitations

  • Physical examination may be limited or incomplete
  • Certain diagnostic tests cannot be performed remotely
  • Provider cannot directly intervene in emergency situations
  • May not be appropriate for all medical conditions

Alternative Options

Alternatives to telehealth services include:

  • In-person consultation and examination
  • Telephone consultation (audio only)
  • Email or secure messaging communication
  • Traditional face-to-face medical care

Privacy and Confidentiality

  • All telehealth sessions are conducted using HIPAA-compliant technology
  • Your medical information will be kept confidential as required by law
  • Sessions may be recorded only with your explicit consent
  • You are responsible for ensuring privacy on your end (private location, secure device)
  • Family members or other individuals should not be present unless you request their participation

Technical Requirements

  • Reliable internet connection
  • Computer, tablet, or smartphone with camera and microphone
  • Updated web browser or mobile app
  • Private, well-lit location for consultation
  • Valid email address for appointment confirmations

Under Arizona Revised Statutes ¬ß 36-3602, telehealth services must meet specific requirements:

  • You must be physically located in Arizona during the consultation
  • Your identity will be verified at the beginning of each session
  • Your location within Arizona will be confirmed
  • This consent is governed by Arizona law
  • Medical records will be maintained according to Arizona regulations

Patient Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate health and contact information
  • Be located in Arizona during all telehealth sessions
  • Ensure privacy and minimize distractions during sessions
  • Have a backup communication method available
  • Follow treatment recommendations and medication instructions
  • Report any technical problems immediately
  • Seek emergency care when appropriate (call 911)

Provider Responsibilities

  • Verify your identity and Arizona location before each session
  • Use secure, HIPAA-compliant technology platforms
  • Maintain professional standards of care
  • Document all telehealth encounters in your medical record
  • Provide clear instructions for follow-up care
  • Be available for urgent questions between scheduled visits
  • Refer to in-person care when clinically indicated

Consent Withdrawal

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent for telehealth services at any time
  • Request in-person consultation instead
  • Stop a telehealth session at any time
  • Ask questions about telehealth services

Withdrawing consent will not affect your right to receive in-person healthcare services.
